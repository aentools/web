About Us

Antenor Neto holds a bachelor's degree in Science and Technology and his training includes a master's and doctoral degree in Chemistry (UFABC). He mainly works with computer science and analytical programming in the Unix environment. Molecular Dynamics techniques and DFT approaches also make up its competence in the field of physical chemistry. He is currently researching MACHINE LEARNING techniques and Data Science tools along with Web development for multidisciplinary applications.
